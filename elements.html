About Me

Sraddha

I am a curious and adventurous person who loves to explore new ideas and experiences. Whether it’s diving into a great book, expressing myself through writing, or discovering new places while traveling, I find joy in learning and broadening my horizons. I have a natural ability to connect with people, combining my creativity and problem-solving skills to make a meaningful impact in my work and personal life. I am always ready to embrace a challenge.

Professionally, I have built a career in data analytics and leadership. I spent nearly three years at L&T Technology Services in India, where I gained deep insights into the industry. After moving to Canada, I earned a Master’s degree in Applied Computer Science from Concordia University and gained two years of Canadian work experience, at Forum Research. Currently, I lead a team of 40-50 people, guiding them through complex market research and data analytics projects. I take pride in fostering collaboration and helping my team grow while delivering impactful results.

My Blog

Beyond my professional achievements, I am a passionate writer who runs her blog on WordPress, Halycon26blog, which I started at the age of 18. My blog reflects my love for storytelling and sharing ideas, resonating with a growing audience. With nearly 1,000 views per post, my writing has connected with readers around the world, showcasing my ability to communicate thoughtfully and authentically. Writing has always been an integral part of my life, offering me a creative outlet and a way to connect with others on a deeper level.
